# Digital Survivorship / Maria Emergency Access Plan

## Scope

Design a secure, authenticated continuity plan so Maria can access necessary household, account, domain, infrastructure, and LifeOps knowledge if Sean dies or becomes incapacitated.

## Status

Dormant but important. Not currently active.

## Critical constraints

- Strong authentication preventing spoofing or social engineering.
- Pre-authorized survivorship workflow, not ad-hoc request-response.
- Identified critical systems and access paths without exposing them in routine context.
- Delay / verification steps before access release.
- Privacy-preserving treatment of items that should remain sealed unless necessary.

## Future scope (not Phase 1)

- Which systems matter
- Where account and recovery information lives
- What Maria needs access to
- How Maria's identity is authenticated
- What approval, delay, or verification steps are required
- What household, family, financial, domain, infrastructure, and operational knowledge would be needed for continuity
- How access is granted without weakening day-to-day security
